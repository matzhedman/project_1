Background

Martin Luther King was born in Atlanta, Georgia, U.S, January 15, 1929. He was the second of three children with one older sister and one younger brother. King married Corretta Scott in June 18, 1953 and they had four children. Martin Luther King was a pastor in the Ebenezer Baptist Church until he was shot to death 4th of April 1968.

Famous for

Martin Luther King is most famous for his speech "I have a dream" that he held at the Lincoln Memorial in 1963. He is also known as a leader of the Civil Rights Movement. In 1964 King recieved the Nobel Peace Prize.

Story about the speech

The speech "I have a dream" was ranked the top American speech in the 20th century. What many may not know is that the most famous part of the speech is an improvisation from mr King. It is said that the gospel singer Mahalia Jackson, at the end of his prepared text, shouted to mr King "Tell them about your dream!", and then he shared his dream which now is known world wide.
